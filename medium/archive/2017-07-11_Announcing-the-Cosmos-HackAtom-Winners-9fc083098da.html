Announcing the Cosmos HackAtom Winners

Thanks to all who participated in our first Cosmos Hackathon!

We are excited to announce the winners, who will receive prizes totaling $50,000 in ETH. More than 50 teams and individuals from around the world have spent the month hacking on Cosmos.

This hackathon is just the start, and we look forward to seeing many more interesting software projects built for Cosmos in the coming months. If you missed the hackathon, didn’t have time to realize your full vision, or are passionate about a big new idea, be sure to connect with us on our community Slack and we’ll help you on your way.

Cosmos Prizes

Grand Prize ($20,000 worth of ETH)

Winner: MultiVerse
Github: https://github.com/multiverseHQ
Stacks: Docker, GoLang, AWS, VueJS, Tendermint, Cosmos
Description: Multiverse makes life easy for the blockchain developer who wants to deploy their Tendermint ABCi blockchain application in one click — like Heroku for blockchain developers.

2nd Grand Prize ($10,000 worth of ETH)

Winner: Scisco
Github: https://github.com/cwgoes/scisco/blob/master/README.md
Stacks: Haskell, ReactJS, Tendermint, ABCI
Description: A privacy-preserving auditable distributed voting system using Linkable Ring Signatures.

3rd Place ($6,000 worth of ETH)

Winner: Block.finance
Github: https://github.com/block-finance/cb-ledger
Stacks: Advanced C++, Tendermint, gRPC, Boost, Cosmos
Description: A secure multi-currency bank ledger that enables frictionless payments and settlements.

4th Place ($4,000 worth of ETH)

Winner: Post Quantum
Github: Post Quantum Whitepaper
Stacks: GoLang, Tendermint
Description: A protocol for adding a post quantum primitive to an existing Proof of Stake primitive in a way that avoids slowing down consensus but preserves integrity into the Quantum computing future.

Cosmos Prizes Judging Process

The Cosmos team first screened all the projects submission for eligibility which were then carefully reviewed by our expert panel of judges Jae Kwon and Ethan Buchman (co-founders of Cosmos).

Our next HackAtom starts on August 5th, 2017. See https://cosmos.network/hackatom for details.
