Cosmos Global HackAtom #2 is Underway

Update: We are extending the deadline for submissions to HackAtom #2 to September 14th in time to make the announcement of winners at the 3rd Annual Global Blockchain Summit in Shanghai.

  • August 5 — September 14
  • $50k in prizes
  • Participate from anywhere in the world

See cosmos.network/hackatom for details and hackathon.io/cosmos to sign up for HackAtom #2.

Join the discussion about #hackatom here: Rocket Chat

About this event

HackAtom #2 is seeking individuals and teams worldwide for a $50k hackathon from August 5 — September 14, 2017. Participants will turn innovative ideas into working applications, with rewards from the Cosmos community.

HackAtom #2 runs for little under six weeks. You’ll build a real product while the crypto and blockchain communities watch and cast their vote on the project that captures their imagination. Tell the story behind your innovative idea, turn it into a working product, get other members to join, and build something cool!

This event is accessible to everyone irrespective of location, gender, age, computing power, etc. Participation is open and global, giving anyone from anywhere an opportunity to exhibit merit and be recognized for it. Demonstrate your creativity. More importantly, have fun.

May the odds be ever in your favor.
