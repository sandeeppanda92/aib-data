We estimate that the Cosmos Hub will be launching short of 3 months’ time. There are a lot of exciting announcements jam packed into this month’s update so here goes.

Tendermint New Hires

Christopher Goes—Software Developer

Christopher Goes hopes that distributed ledgers will shift the economics of network effects in favor of aligned incentives and positive-sum games. He plans to help Cosmos make this happen by developing robust software infrastructure layers and reducing barriers to entry. Christopher previously cofounded Stash.ai and led CUAUV.

David Braun—Software Developer

David is a full-stack generalist with broad experience of diverse languages, platforms, and technologies including specialization in Node.js and 2 years of blockchain experience. He created Deterministic.js, a deterministic version of JavaScript for writing distributed smart contract applications. David designed Eris Worker, a distributed computation framework designed to support autonomous operations on blockchains. David is working on the Cosmos-UI, codenamed: Voyager.

Zaki Manian—Head of Tendermint Labs

Zaki Manian, Executive Director of Trusted IoT Alliance, is a prolific contributor to the development of blockchain and cryptocurrency technology. Zaki has deep expertise in cryptography and distributed consensus systems. He is an advisor to the Cosmos project and several other investment funds and startups in the space. Zaki is now joining us as the Director of Tendermint Labs: a proprietary incubator for Cosmos-enabled business models with Tendermint.

Ethereum Community Fund

The Cosmos project and the initiatives behind it, driven by the spirit of open-source, is built upon the theme of nurturing education, robust growth, backed by fundamentals through infrastructure, community, and research development.

We’re backing the grant initiatives of the Ethereum Community Fund, as the program has aligned objectives consistent with the ethos at Cosmos. We believe in enriching the soil that the nascent blockchain industry is dependent upon by donating grants to promising infrastructure, research, and philanthropic endeavors. We believe that by doing so today will bear implications for broader cryptocurrency adoption later as well as lend welfare to positive social impact.

Notable Projects Using Tendermint

OmiseGO DEX

Yesterday, OMG announced a lighthearted but factual blog post on April Fool’s Day announcing a non-contentious but rather, “consensual”, as they put it, hard spoon. Well, this confirms it. OMG is, in fact, spooning the balances of the OMG ERC20 token into the genesis state of their DEX built on top of the Tendermint consensus engine. The spooned token will be used as a staking token on the DEX zone, which will connect to the Cosmos ecosystem over IBC.

The link to their Honte-daemon open-source repository, once public, is here:

  • https://github.com/omisego/honted

The question remains though: Who is the big spoon and who is the little spoon? We’ll leave it up to the readers’ imaginations to decide.
