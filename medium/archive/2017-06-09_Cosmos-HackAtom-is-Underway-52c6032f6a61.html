What sort of ideas do we want to see built?

We’d love to see anything you can dream up! This hackathon is not limited to Cosmos or even Tendermint. We’ve provided some sample ABCI apps below to give you a sense of what’s possible.

  • Permissioned Blocks
    One of the winner of Consensus 2017 Hackathon built a solution to help farmers in Africa. The team combined both Ethermint and IPFS. They benefited from using the Tendermint consensus engine rather than the vanilla Ethereum POW to achieve higher throughput and lower gas fees. See the following technical white paper about the design. It's a P2P file sharing with permissions but also has the ability to make smart contract state information private, see https://github.com/tendermint/ethermint
  • Seigniorage Shares
    Next-generation cryptocurrencies should incorporate an elastic supply rule that adjusts the quantity of coin supply proportionately to changes in coin market value. Please see this white paper for more information. This can be implemented using Basecoin, a cryptocurrency framework in Golang, see https://github.com/tendermint/basecoin
  • High Frequency Energy Trading
    Tendermint supports a much higher transaction throughput than most other blockchains. The energy market is ripe for disruption and we’d love to see whether you can build an app that allows traders to quickly exchange energy. This can be implemented as an ABCI app or on top of Basecoin or Ethermint. See https://enerchain.ponton.de
  • App that tracks shares and ownership of companies
    Tracking who owns which shares is a hassle in any company, and it is important to keep and accurate and trusted record of that information. It should be possible to create new shares for a new company, as well as move shares between different accounts.
  • Voting/governance systems
    A mobile voting platform designed to securely cast votes in elections across the globe.
  • E-voting for board rooms
    Company boards have to meet twice a year to vote on important issues. Can you think of a way to move this process online with Tendermint. Is it possible to implement delegated voting?
  • Trading one type of coin for another
    Building a DEX or decentralised exchange is one of the holy grails of applications for blockchains. Can you implement something similar to this whitepaper on top of Tendermint/Ethermint?
  • Digitizing physical assets such as silver
    Trading gold and silver is a cumbersome process. Traditionally you need to rent a warehouse, provide security and ensure safe transport. Can you build an app that allows for the easy trading of many physical assets such as gold?
  • Tracking physical goods
    From tracking conflict diamonds to preventing medication to be tampered with, the blockchain has the potential to provide a unique source of truth. Can you build a system that allows buyers to verify the chain of custody and the origin of certain products?

What are the judging criteria?

Great work of all kinds will be considered for the awards. In particular, our judges will be looking for apps that excel in these qualities:

  1. Creativity
    Find new use cases, use Cosmos/Tendermint features, integrate with external services, and push the limits of the ABCI apps to make something wonderful.
  2. Positivity
    Make apps that have mainstream appeal and bring useful and understandable value. Apps that empower, entertain, and connect people, and that shine a positive and constructive light on Cosmos/Tendermint as a whole.
  3. Execution
    Make apps that are functional and ready for newcomers to try out. We’re not expecting complete polish or perfection, but would love to see apps that have given consideration to the user experience.
