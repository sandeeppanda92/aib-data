News

OpenMined to switch to Ethermint

The OpenMined project is using Ethermint to build decentralized artificial intelligence on top of the EVM. They are using Ethermint instead of Ethereum due to savings on the gas cost of executing their smart contracts; it is much more expensive to run smart contracts in Ethereum than it is in Cosmos. OpenMined chose to switch networks because Ethermint allows them to raise the maximum gas per block and also run logic faster due to Tendermint’s higher throughput of 200 transactions per second (thanks to our Proof-of-Stake security model).

Validator Testnet

Good news is, the validator testnet went live mid-October! Bad news is, we ran into some bumps along the way. Too many people were connecting to a single node, which didn’t bode well for our testnet (oops). Fixes are underway and the testnet will be up and running again. Meanwhile, active community channels have moved from Rocket Chat!
