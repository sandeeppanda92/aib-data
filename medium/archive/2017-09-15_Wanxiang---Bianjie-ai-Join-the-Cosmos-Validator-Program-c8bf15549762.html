WanCloud Joins the Cosmos Validator Program

September 14

We are very excited to announce that one of the largest conglomerates in China, Wanxiang, is participating in the Cosmos Validator Program. The Cosmos Network, powered by Tendermint, is the industry leader at the bleeding edge of blockchain technology, taking Proof-of-Stake beyond just a Proof-of-Concept into production. Today, WanCloud, a division of Wanxiang, is pioneering the blockchain space in China by signing up as one of the first participants in our Validator Program.

“WanCloud focuses on promoting blockchain technology and its applications in China. We are very excited that more people are able to use Cosmos through WanCloud’s platform. We will work closely with Cosmos to develop an open blockchain ecosystem and promote interchain technology and commercial adoption in China.” — Tom Tao, WanCloud GM

Cosmos Validators act as the mechanism that secures the Cosmos Network. The Cosmos Network is designed to be the “Internet of Blockchains,” envisioning an interoperable multi-chain network which provides the means for trustless exchange of cryptographic assets across independent blockchains, called zones, through a master hub chain known as the Cosmos Hub.

In order to become a validator and have some positive amount of voting power, validators must lock up a predetermined number of tokens. This can be self-funded, else, voting power can be obtained by having other staking token holders “delegate” stake to a validator.

“Proof-of-Stake and Internet Scale Byzantine fault-tolerance (BFT) is one of the most exciting frontiers in blockchain networks. Proof-of-Stake offers the possibility of massively higher blockchain throughput. New expertise is needed to achieve secure high throughput networks. I’m excited to be working with the Cosmos Foundation to advise Cosmos stakeholders about what validator choices will contribute towards the strongest network for the entire industry.” — Zaki Manian, Tendermint Advisor
